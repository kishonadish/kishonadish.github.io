Dave Dayman

I have been playing guitar since the age of 9. My first guitar was a beaten up acoustic that my dad could play three chords on (that was ok as he was and still is a big Status Quo fan).  Originally the idea was to play in a heavy metal band (very uncool at the time as it was 1982 and the electronica movement was taking over the air waves).  I can still remember the feeling of complete joy as I unwrapped the cellophane from the Number of the Beast record (yes – record! Good old vinyl) and then tentatively putting the needle onto the spinning plastic disc to hear the boom of Dickinson and the Maiden boys resonating from my cheap speakers.  Ah, childhood….
 
From that point on I was hooked on anything to do with music and in particular the guitar and have always had a passion for rock based music.  My taste has grown over the years (age and wisdom do that to you apparently) and my influences include a wide range of artists from Queen to Muse, Band of Skulls to Biffy Clyro, Simple Minds to Early Maiden, Pink Floyd to Free and plenty in between.  I have played with various bands over the years and have a wide repertoire.

These days I can be found playing for the North West/North Wales based band HOODWINK.  And I am loving being part of this brilliant five-piece band.
