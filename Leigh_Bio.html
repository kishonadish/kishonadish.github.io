Leigh Wilson

Leigh Wilson has been singing for a number of years with various groups but has been spending more and more time recently as lead vocalist with Hoodwink.

With a wide range, he is able to cope with the large setlist Hoodwink pride themselves in playing. Anyone who can take on a Queen song with ease the way Leigh does is someone who has to be taken seriously.

Not only is he a superb singer, but he also plays a mean guitar which enhances the bands' sound. This allows the band to get that "near perfect" rendition for each song.
